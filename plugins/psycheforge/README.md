# PsycheForge

**A self-transformation studio for mental reprogramming of your own patterns** — beliefs,
habits, fears, states, identity narratives. Fifteen units: one director, two gates with
veto power, twelve craft specialists across six registers (cognitive, behavioral,
hypnotic, imaginal, somatic, narrative), staged on the alchemical Magnum Opus in Jung's
psychological reading.

## What it does

You bring a pattern of your own mind ("I freeze when I have to negotiate", "doomscrolling
eats my evenings", "the voice that says I'm an impostor"). The studio:

1. takes the case (the-anamnesist) and screens it for safety (the-gatekeeper — real STOP
   authority: crisis → routed to real help, no protocol);
2. maps the pattern's mechanics — loops, payoffs, secondary gains, leverage points
   (the-cartographer);
3. stages it on the opus — nigredo → albedo → citrinitas → rubedo — and picks the
   program's dramaturgy (the-alchemist);
4. forges interventions in the registers the staging calls for (cognitive-smith,
   habit-wright, suggestionist, imaginalist, somatist, reauthor);
5. wraps them in runnable ritual (the-ritualist) and compiles ONE program — daily cards,
   measures, kill criteria, optional WaveForge audio spec (the-weaver);
6. audits it (the-trialmaster, 12-point checklist) and lints claims (the-gatekeeper);
7. tracks it across weeks and iterates on evidence (the-ledger).

## Modes

- **FULL OPUS** — 28-day staged program
- **QUICK REWIRE** — one narrow belief/habit, 7 days
- **SHADOW SESSION** — single-session symbolic/imaginal deep-dive with rails
- **CHECK-IN** — adherence + measures → adjust one variable
- **Standalone** — any specialist directly, always wrapped by the gates

Start with: *"reprogramare mentală"*, *"vreau să scap de..."*, *"rewire my..."*,
*"shadow work"*, *"check-in pe protocol"* — or just describe the pattern.

## Honesty architecture

Every technique carries an evidence label: `[EVIDENCE-BASED]` `[MODERATE-EVIDENCE]`
`[TRADITIONAL-SYMBOLIC]` `[EXPERIMENTAL]`. The alchemical corpus is used as Jung read
it — a symbolic map of psychic transformation; symbols work through meaning, attention,
expectation and ritual structure, and the deliverables say so. The knowledge base
(`skills/psycheforge/references/`, 8 files) is research-backed with real, dated sources —
including the unglamorous findings: affirmations can backfire at low self-esteem (Wood
2009), subliminals don't work (Greenwald 1991), positive fantasy alone reduces effort
(Oettingen), the Kybalion is from 1908.
