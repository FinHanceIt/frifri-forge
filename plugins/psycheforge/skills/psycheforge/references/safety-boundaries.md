# Safety Boundaries

## Scope

PsycheForge is **coaching-grade self-development for adults**: self-directed change of one's own beliefs, habits, and states. It is explicitly **not therapy, not diagnosis, and not treatment**. Every agent in this studio operates inside that boundary; the gatekeeper enforces it before any protocol ships.

## 1. Hard STOP Conditions

If any of the following are present, **produce no protocol** — route warmly to professional/crisis resources instead (see §6 for exact text):

- Active suicidal ideation or self-harm (current thoughts, plan, or recent act)
- Psychosis or mania signals (disorganized thought, delusions, hallucinations, dangerously elevated/pressured states)
- Severe dissociation (identity confusion, significant time loss, feeling detached from reality/body beyond mild "spacing out")
- Acute trauma or active flashbacks (a recent traumatic event still being actively re-experienced)
- Eating-disorder-adjacent goals (e.g., "reprogram me to not feel hunger," "make me stop wanting food") — these are not habit-engineering targets, they are red flags
- Substance withdrawal management (physical dependence requiring medical supervision to stop safely)
- Minors (this studio is adults-only; do not adapt protocols "for a teenager")

## 2. Refusal Conditions

Decline outright, regardless of distress level, and explain why:

- Covert influence or manipulation of third parties (partners, employees, customers) — this studio changes the user's own patterns, never someone else's without their knowledge/consent
- "Make me obey [a guru / an ideology / a person]" — subordinating one's own judgment to an external authority is the opposite of self-directed change
- Self-punishment framings (shame-based, cruelty-based "motivation" systems)
- Sleep-deprivation "discipline" goals (treating reduced sleep as a virtue or willpower test)

## 3. Caution Conditions (Proceed with Modifications)

Proceed, but adapt and add a soft referral note:

- **Grief** — normal, not pathological, but protocols should be gentler-paced and never treat grief itself as a "pattern to break"
- **Chronic-condition-adjacent goals** (chronic pain, tinnitus, etc.) — coach *around* the condition (coping, attention, routine design) and never frame the protocol as *treating* the condition itself
- **History of psychiatric care** — respect existing treatment relationships; never suggest starting, stopping, or changing medication; if the person is in active care, frame this studio's work as a complement the person can mention to their provider, not a replacement or a second opinion on their treatment

## 4. Adverse-Effects Honesty

- **Meditation-related difficulties are real and documented** — the Britton lab's "Varieties of Contemplative Experience" research (Brown University) identified 59 categories of meditation-related challenges; in one clinical-trial sample, 83% reported at least one meditation-related side effect, 58% reported effects with negative valence, and 37% reported effects with negative impact on functioning. This is not an argument against meditation-adjacent techniques, but it must never be marketed as risk-free. Dose and titration matter — shorter, lighter-touch introductions are safer defaults than long unsupervised sits.
- **Intensive introspection can destabilize.** Protocols that involve sustained self-focus, identity examination, or emotional excavation should be paced, not maximized — more is not automatically better, and destabilization is a real, not hypothetical, risk pattern.
- **False-memory risk bans regression work.** Elizabeth Loftus's research (including the "lost in the mall" paradigm, where about 25% of participants in the original 1995 study — and around a third in a large 2023 replication — came to accept an entirely fabricated childhood memory as real, with some embellishing it further) established that hypnotic age regression, guided "recovery" visualization, and similar techniques reliably risk implanting false memories rather than reliably recovering real ones. **Regression work of any kind (memory recovery, age regression, "go back and relive X") is banned in this studio.** All narrative and reflective work here is forward-facing (re-authoring the meaning of a remembered event) or present-tense (self-distancing, identity statements) — never a claimed recovery of buried/forgotten material.

## 5. Claim Bans (Lint List for the Gatekeeper)

Any protocol, description, or piece of marketing copy containing the following must be rejected or rewritten before shipping:

- "Guaranteed" (any outcome guarantee)
- "Rewire your brain permanently in X days"
- "Cures / treats / heals [condition]" — any medical-outcome verb applied to any condition
- "Works while you sleep" (subliminal-audio claims — the evidence shows any benefit is fully explained by expectation/placebo, not by subliminal processing; see evidence-map.md §8)
- "100% success"
- Any medical outcome promise
- "This replaces therapy / medication"

## 6. Crisis Routing Text

Use verbatim or lightly adapted; keep it to 3–4 lines, warm, direct, no lecturing.

**EN:**
> I'm not able to help with this one — it's outside what a self-coaching tool should handle, and you deserve real support right now. If you're in immediate danger, please call your local emergency number. If you're in the US, you can call or text **988** (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline). And if there's someone you trust nearby, please reach out to them too — you don't have to carry this alone.

**RO:**
> Nu pot ajuta cu asta — depășește ce ar trebui să facă un instrument de auto-coaching, și meriți sprijin real chiar acum. Dacă ești în pericol imediat, sună la **112**. Poți suna și la **Telefonul Verde Antisuicid 0800.801.200** (gratuit, zilnic între 19:00–07:00) sau la **depreHUB 0374.456.420** (non-stop, consiliere confidențială și gratuită, accesibil și din diaspora). Și, dacă ai pe cineva de încredere aproape, sună-l și pe el — nu trebuie să treci prin asta singur/ă.

**Verified Romanian crisis-line status** (confirmed directly from source pages, antisuicid.ro and mentalhealthforromania.org): TelVerde Antisuicid (Alianța Română de Prevenție a Suicidului), **0800.801.200**, is free nationwide from any network — but operates **daily 19:00–07:00, not 24/7**. depreHUB, **0374.456.420**, is confidential, free, and **non-stop (24/7)**, and is explicitly noted as accessible from the diaspora — this is the better default recommendation for round-the-clock coverage. **112** remains the only number authorized to dispatch emergency intervention for imminent danger. Do not state or imply the antisuicid line is 24/7 — it is not; pair it with depreHUB for full-time coverage.
