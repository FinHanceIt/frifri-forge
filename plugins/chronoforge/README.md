# ChronoForge

**A new way to connect to the future — built honestly.**
An Esoteric-Seeded, Science-Locked (ESL) event-prediction studio. 27 agents. For Fri / FriFri Creatives.

---

## The idea in one paragraph

You cannot receive information from the future — no tachyon, no entanglement signal, no precognition; that physics is settled. But you *can* read the present far better than most, and much of the esoterica you named (Reality Transurfing, quantum-jumping, synchronicity), stripped of its metaphysics, is a crude pointer at **real, measurable phenomena** conventional forecasting under-uses — attention cascades, reflexivity, expectation loops. ChronoForge admits those framings as **hypothesis generators**, forces each into a **measurable present-signal proxy**, locks every proxy against physics, forecasts with proven calibrated machinery, and keeps a **rolling Brier score per lens** so Transurfing — and every other lens — earns its weight or gets killed. Belief is never an input; falsification is the judge.

## Why it's different

Every serious forecaster is science-locked at the back. Almost none has a *disciplined, falsifiable front-end* for lateral and fringe hypotheses. ChronoForge does — and the **Lens Ledger** is the invention: it turns "I took Reality Transurfing into account" from self-deception into a self-correcting research instrument.

## The pipeline

```
FRAME → DIVERGE (lenses) → OPERATIONALIZE (gate) → REALITY-LOCK (science council)
      → [INVENT] → GATHER & TRIANGULATE → FORECAST (ensemble) → CALIBRATE & FALSIFY (gate)
      → SCORE LENSES (ledger) → ADVERSARY → SYNTHESIZE → REMEMBER
```

Three hard gates (operationalization, reality-lock, calibration) and two human checkpoints (confirm the question; approve any actionable call).

## What it honestly promises

On a well-posed question with real data, ChronoForge will: generate a wider, stranger, better-covered set of candidate drivers than a conventional pipeline; force every one to be measurable and physically possible; produce a calibrated probability with honest intervals and a stated predictability horizon; and keep score forever, so the system and its fringe lenses get better or get cut. It will **never** claim to see the future.

## How to use

In Claude Code / Cowork with this plugin installed:
- *"ChronoForge: will [X] happen by [date]?"* → full run.
- *"Frame this prediction question."* → `question-framer` only.
- *"Brainstorm drivers for [X] through the lenses."* → DIVERGE only.
- *"Ledger review."* → which lenses are earning vs killed.
- *"Is [framing] physically possible / what method fits [signal]?"* → `physics-gate` / `math-complexity-scientist`.

## Reuse

Built on your existing stack — it does not rebuild what works:
- **Project Oracle** (`predictive-agents`): triangulator, Bayesian event-modeler, calibrator, validator.
- **TrendForge**: lead-lag correlator, emergence-predictor, chain forecaster.
- **ChronoForge's delta**: the lens front-end, the operationalization gate, the science-lock, and the Lens Ledger.

## Honesty boundary

No fabricated data, sources, numbers, or track records. A backtest is never a live record. Forward inference only. Esoteric lenses are on probation until the ledger says otherwise — and every report says so.
