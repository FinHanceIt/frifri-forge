# Research Brief 01 — Esoteric Corpus → Formalizable Heuristics

> Evidence-honest mining of "reality/consciousness" esoterica for a prediction system.
> Three registers are kept strictly separate: **[CLAIM]** what proponents assert · **[EVIDENCE]** what the record shows · **[REFRAME]** an analytical mapping (mine, *not* the proponent's claim). Every factual assertion is cited. No parapsychology result is stated as settled; replication status is always attached.

---

## 1. Reality Transurfing (Vadim Zeland)

Zeland presents Transurfing as resting on quantum physics + parallel worlds, but it "bears only superficial resemblance to quantum mechanical concepts like superposition and measurement" and extends them "into metaphysical territory without empirical support" — New Age / quantum-mysticism. Zeland himself says the techniques don't require accepting his model, i.e. he makes no scientific-validity claim. Nothing in the [CLAIM] rows is validated; only the [REFRAME] rows carry forward, each standing on independent peer-reviewed / market-practitioner literature. ([The Path to Awesomeness](https://thepathtoawesomeness.com/reality-transurfing/); [Dream Life NZ](https://www.dreamlifenz.com/post/reality-transurfing-and-quantum-mechanics))

### 1a. Pendulums (egregors / thought-energy structures)
- **[CLAIM]** When many people think alike, their "thought waves" superimpose into an autonomous "energy-information structure" (pendulum) that feeds on attention and pushes people toward goals not their own; the defense is to *ignore* it. Overlaps with the occult "egregore." ([Zeland – Pendulum](https://vadimzeland.medium.com/pendulum-4fbb30e31c18); [Egregores – Bluwr](https://bluwr.com/en/p/35028214))
- **[EVIDENCE]** Unfalsifiable. No measurable "energy-information structure"; borrows wave/field vocabulary with none of the physics.
- **[REFRAME]** The real phenomenon underneath is **information cascades / herding** (Bikhchandani-Hirshleifer-Welch 1992; Banerjee 1992), which measurably raises system fragility. ([Herd Behavior – IMF PDF](https://www.imf.org/external/pubs/ft/staffp/2001/01/pdf/Bikhchan.pdf); [Cascades survey](https://tamuz.caltech.edu/papers/cascades_survey.pdf)) **Proxy:** social-volume z-score, search/pageview spike ratios, engagement-percentile virality thresholds. "Pendulum gaining strength" ≈ a positive-feedback attention cascade you can chart.

### 1b. Excess potential
- **[CLAIM]** Inflated *importance* creates an "energy imbalance" that summons "equalizing forces" which deflate what you inflated — "the more important the goal, the harder to reach."
- **[EVIDENCE]** Unfalsifiable — no units, no mechanism; any outcome retrofits. Thin legitimate kernel: choking-under-pressure is real; the cosmic-equilibrium framing is not.
- **[REFRAME]** Best analogue: **crowd over-commitment / positioning extremes that mean-revert** — the core of contrarian analysis. **Proxy:** crypto perpetual **funding rates** at extremes (crowded side vulnerable to liquidation cascades — almost literally "the inflated side gets deflated"), open interest, short interest. ([Funding rates & reversals – Yellow](https://yellow.com/learn/how-to-read-funding-rates-crypto-reversals))

### 1c. Importance (inner / outer)
- **[CLAIM]** Importance generates excess potential; outer importance (usually carrying fear) tends to manifest the dreaded result. Prescription: reduce importance.
- **[EVIDENCE]** Unfalsifiable as a cosmic law; confirmation-bias-friendly. Practical core (lower the stakes to reduce anxiety) is folk psychology dressed as mechanism.
- **[REFRAME]** Strongest analogue: **Soros's reflexivity + sentiment overreaction/mean-reversion** — perceptions of value feed back into prices until they snap back. ([Reflexivity – Alpha Architect](https://alphaarchitect.com/reflexivity-and-the-feedback-effect-in-financial-markets/); [Soros](https://www.georgesoros.com/2014/01/13/fallibility-reflexivity-and-the-human-uncertainty-principle-2/)) **Proxy:** put/call ratio at extremes, fear/greed indices, VIX, social/search surges. **Guardrail:** extremes can *persist* — a conditioning variable, not a timing trigger.

### 1d. The Space of Variations
- **[CLAIM]** All realities pre-exist as "sectors"; you don't build reality, you *select* which sector you resonate with.
- **[EVIDENCE]** Metaphysical, unfalsifiable. Appropriates Many-Worlds vocabulary, but MWI does not let an observer "select" a branch.
- **[REFRAME]** Defensible analogue: the **probabilistic scenario / state-space view** — the future is a distribution of paths; forecasting is assigning/updating probabilities (ensemble forecasting, Monte-Carlo path fans, 80/95% intervals). "Choosing a lifeline" honestly recast = shifting the probability mass you condition on via new evidence (a Bayesian update). **Proxy:** prediction-interval coverage, scenario weights, prediction-market probabilities as an externally-priced "space of variations."

### 1e. Outer vs inner intention
- **[CLAIM]** Inner intention = resoluteness to *act*; outer intention = resoluteness to *have*, where the goal "realizes by itself."
- **[EVIDENCE]** The "inner attitude reorganizes the external world" half is manifestation relabeled; the inner half (goal-directed action) is trivially real.
- **[REFRAME]** Useful structure: separate **forceable levers you control (inner)** from **environmental state you can only align with / time (outer)** — riding an exogenous trend vs fighting the tape. **Proxy:** trend/momentum & regime detection, **lead-lag indicators** (act on an upstream signal so the downstream "arrives by itself"). Operational nugget: grade every signal as *forceable* vs *timing-only*.

### 1f. Slides / visualization
- **[CLAIM]** Systematic visualization of the goal-already-achieved "materializes the corresponding sector."
- **[EVIDENCE]** Unfalsifiable and unsupported — and outcome-fantasy can *reduce* effort, cutting against the claim. Surviving kernel: mental rehearsal shapes attention/behavior.
- **[REFRAME]** **Expectation/priming and self-fulfilling loops** — shared expectations become self-fulfilling (expected recession alters spending; expected bank run triggers one). **Proxy:** expectation indices (consumer confidence, PMI-expectations), analyst-consensus revisions, prediction-market probabilities — model expectations as a leading input distinct from fundamentals.

## 2. Quantum Jumping / Reality Shifting / "Quantum Dimensions"

**Bottom line up front:** No physics supports jumping between branches/"dimensions" by intention. "Quantum" is branding, not mechanism. Any real effect is psychological (attention, absorption, expectancy, identity priming) — and only those are measurable.

- **[CLAIM] Burt Goldman's Quantum Jumping** (Mindvalley): infinite alternate-universe versions of you; through visualization you "jump" and "download" their skills. ([Mindvalley blog](https://blog.mindvalley.com/quantum-jumping/))
- **[CLAIM] Reality-shifting subculture** (#shifttok): move consciousness from "CR" to a scripted "DR"; many believe they literally enter another universe. Surged 2020. ([Screenshot Media](https://screenshot-media.com/culture/internet-culture/reality-shifting-tiktok/))

**[EVIDENCE] — why physics does not support it:**
- **Quantum mysticism / "quantum woo"** — spirituality linked to QM by coincidental language. ([Quantum mysticism – Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quantum_mysticism); [Quantum Jumping – RationalWiki](https://rationalwiki.org/wiki/Quantum_Jumping))
- **Decoherence** blocks macroscopic superposition; Tegmark computed brain decoherence ~10⁻¹³–10⁻²⁰ s vs neural ~10⁻³–10⁻¹ s — "classical rather than quantum system." ([Tegmark – arXiv quant-ph/9907009](https://arxiv.org/abs/quant-ph/9907009))
- **No-communication theorem** forbids using entanglement to bias a distant outcome. ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/No-communication_theorem))
- **Even under Many-Worlds**, branches decohere into non-interacting worlds you cannot cross or select. ([Carroll & Sebens – arXiv 1405.7907](https://arxiv.org/abs/1405.7907))

**[HONEST KERNEL]** These are immersive-imagination + autosuggestion exercises. Real effects: structured **absorption** (Tellegen Absorption Scale), adjacency to **maladaptive daydreaming** with matching risks (Somer et al., *Current Psychology* 2021), **expectancy/placebo**, **identity priming**. ([Somer et al. – PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/34744401/)) **Proxy:** model the *person* — absorption (TAS) + maladaptive-daydreaming severity (**MDS-16**; the ~40 suspected-clinical cutoff per the MDS-16 validation, [J. Personality Assessment 2019](https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/full/10.1080/00223891.2019.1594240)) + expectancy + pre/post self-efficacy → measurable behavior change. Hard-code the physics: P(genuine inter-branch transfer) ≈ 0.

## 3. Parapsychology / Precognition Research (handle with most care)

**Read every effect size with its replication status. No result here is accepted as settled by mainstream science.**

### 3a. Daryl Bem, "Feeling the Future" (2011)
- **[CLAIM]** 9 experiments, mean **d ≈ 0.22**, future stimuli influencing prior responses. ([Bem 2011 – JPSP PDF](https://www.apa.org/pubs/journals/features/psp-a0021524.pdf))
- **[EVIDENCE]** Did not replicate. **Ritchie, Wiseman & French (2012)**: 3 pre-registered replications, all null (combined p = .83). **Galak et al. (2012)**: n = 3,289, average **d = 0.04**, indistinguishable from zero. A flagship case of the replication crisis. ([PLOS One](https://journals.plos.org/plosone/article?id=10.1371%2Fjournal.pone.0033423); [Galak – PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/22924750/)) Wagenmakers et al. (2011) Bayesian reanalysis: evidence "weak to nonexistent." ([PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/21280965/))

### 3b. Presentiment / Predictive Anticipatory Activity
- **[CLAIM]** Physiology shifts seconds before a random arousing stimulus. Mossbridge et al. (2012) meta: **~0.21** (CI 0.13–0.29); 2018 update **~0.28**. ([Frontiers](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/psychology/articles/10.3389/fpsyg.2012.00390/full))
- **[EVIDENCE]** Small, disputed. **Expectation bias from stimulus-sequence dependencies** can masquerade as presentiment; direct multi-measure tests found the expectation confound, not the anomaly. ([PMC critique](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC4598572/))

### 3c. GCP & PEAR
- **[CLAIM]** RNG deviations correlated with global events (GCP); intention nudges RNGs to >7σ over billions of trials (PEAR). ([PEAR – Psi Encyclopedia](https://psi-encyclopedia.spr.ac.uk/articles/princeton-engineering-anomalies-research-pear/))
- **[EVIDENCE] Not accepted.** GCP: post-hoc event selection, flexible parameters. PEAR: weak methodology, misused statistics; independent replications null; lab closed 2007, findings "largely rejected." A tiny effect × astronomical N producing high sigma is the signature of undetected bias, not signal. ([GCP – Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Global_Consciousness_Project); [PEAR – Skeptic's Dictionary](https://www.skepdic.com/pear.html))

### 3d. Ganzfeld
- **[CLAIM]** Storm, Tressoldi & Di Risio (2010): 32.2% hit vs 25% chance, p < .001. ([Psi Encyclopedia](https://psi-encyclopedia.spr.ac.uk/articles/ganzfeld-esp/))
- **[EVIDENCE]** Hyman (2010): mixes flawed studies, inconsistent across labs, "contradictory and elusive." Unresolved since Honorton-Hyman (1980s). ([reply to Hyman – PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/20565166/))

### 3e. CIA Stargate / remote viewing
- **[EVIDENCE]** AIR 1995 (Mumford, Rose & Goslin): Utts saw an effect, Hyman "premature"; AIR found no *operationally useful* intelligence. CIA terminated the ~$20M program. Not established. ([Remote viewing – Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Remote_viewing))

### Honest bottom line
No replicable, mechanism-backed precognition signal is accepted by mainstream science. Claimed effects are tiny (d ≈ 0.04–0.28), contested, shadowed by publication bias and expectation-bias confounds; the flagship 2011 result failed pre-registered replication.

**The extractable gift is the *methodology*, not the findings.** In a low-prior domain, serious practitioners and critics forged exactly the anti-self-deception tools our system should steal: **pre-registration** of hypotheses and analysis; **randomization, blinding, automation**; **Bayesian model comparison with explicit priors**; **adversarial collaboration / a built-in skeptic** (Utts vs Hyman on one dataset); **effect-size + CI reporting** and **publication-bias-robust meta-analysis**. The lesson: a tiny effect × huge N producing "high sigma" is a red flag — bias-robustness must be a first-class gate.

## 4. Synchronicity, I Ching, Astrology, Tarot

**As prediction: unsupported.** Synchronicity ≈ apophenia + confirmation bias + frequency illusion. Astrology fails controlled tests — **Carlson (*Nature* 1985)** "found no support"; inter-astrologer agreement ~0.1. Why it *feels* accurate: **Barnum/Forer effect**. Tarot/I Ching have no divinatory validity. ([Carlson 1985 – Nature](https://www.nature.com/articles/318419a0); [Barnum effect – Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Barnum_effect))

**The extractable mechanism: structured randomness + projective hypothesis-generation.** These are ambiguous random stimuli that force novel framings — a validated ideation move independent of belief. Tarot is analyzed clinically as a **projective technique** (Semetsky 2006); the secular analogue is **Oblique Strategies** (Eno & Schmidt 1975); the mechanism is de Bono's **Random Input / lateral thinking** breaking functional fixedness. Rominger et al. (PLOS One 2024) found perceiving meaningful coincidences correlates with real-life *creative achievement*. ([Oblique Strategies – Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oblique_Strategies); [Rominger 2024](https://journals.plos.org/plosone/article?id=10.1371%2Fjournal.pone.0300121)) **Use strictly as a random-constraint hypothesis-seed generator, never a signal**, and A/B test whether seeded hypotheses raise candidate diversity/coverage without hurting calibration (Brier ≥ baseline).

## 5. The Extractable Pattern (summary)

| Esoteric concept | Honest status | Measurable proxy | Test vs baseline |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Pendulums** (egregors) | Metaphor; *herding* = Verified | Social-volume z-score, search/pageview spikes, virality percentile | Does a cascade-detector beat naive persistence on continuation/reversal? |
| **Excess potential** | Unsupported as stated; *crowd over-commitment* = Verified | Funding rates, OI, short interest at extremes | Do positioning extremes predict elevated reversal vs base rate? |
| **Importance** | Unsupported as law; *reflexivity/overreaction* = Verified (contested magnitude) | Put/call, fear/greed, VIX, social volume | Does fading extremes beat buy-and-hold out-of-sample? (extremes persist → conditioner) |
| **Space of variations** | Metaphor; *probabilistic state-space* = standard | Prediction intervals, scenario weights, market probs | Interval calibration: do 80% intervals cover ~80%? Brier vs point baseline |
| **Outer/inner intention** | Outer half Unsupported; *controllable-vs-timing / trend-riding* = Verified | Momentum/regime, lead-lag indicators, explore/exploit tags | Do trend-aligned entries beat counter-trend? Does the leader beat the follower? |
| **Slides / visualization** | Unsupported; *expectation/self-fulfilling loops* = Verified | Expectation indices, consensus revisions, market probs | Do expectation shifts lead outcome shifts (Granger)? Do they cut forecast error? |
| **Quantum jumping / shifting** | Physical claim ruled out; *absorption/expectancy/priming* = Verified psychology | TAS, MDS-16, expectancy/self-efficacy | Does identity priming change behavior vs control? (P(branch-jump) ≈ 0) |
| **Precognition / RNG / ganzfeld / RV** | Contested→Unsupported; tiny, failed pre-reg replication, **not accepted** | *No accepted signal.* Extract the **methodology** | Test the methods: does pre-registration + mandatory skeptic + bias-robustness cut false positives? |
| **Synchronicity / astrology / tarot / I Ching** | As prediction Unsupported; as ideation, mechanism Verified | Random-constraint hypothesis seeds | A/B: do seeded hypotheses raise coverage without hurting Brier? |

### Design takeaways
1. One real spine underlies most "reality-selection" esoterica: **reflexive, self-reinforcing collective behavior that mean-reverts at extremes** — carry the measurable crowd/sentiment/expectation proxies, discard the metaphysics.
2. Two RT concepts map to **modeling posture, not signals**: treat the future as a distribution; separate forceable levers from timing-only state.
3. The single most valuable steal is parapsychology's **methodology** (pre-registration, blinding, explicit-prior Bayes, mandatory skeptic, bias-robustness).
4. Divination's only legitimate use is **controlled random-constraint ideation**, quarantined behind the same evidence gate.
5. **Register discipline must survive into the system's own docs:** never import a proponent's causal claim — only the [REFRAME] proxies, each independently cited and each required to beat a baseline before it earns weight.
