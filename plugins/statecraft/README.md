# Statecraft — The Virtual State

A complete government you can run in a chat. Statecraft simulates the machinery of a whole society end to end: one **director skill** convenes the state, and **24 specialist agents** fill every seat — the head of state and head of government, a full cabinet, an independent central bank, parliament and its opposition, a constitutional court, plus the estates that keep power honest: an independent audit, a free press, and the citizenry itself.

It runs as **simulation and analysis**, not as a real-world operations manual. The regime is configurable, but **checks and balances are on by default**, and three gates guard every session: a human charter gate, a human enactment gate, and a hard integrity/safety review.

## What it's for

- **Real policy work** — draft a law, cost it, war-game the consequences, map who wins and who loses, stress-test it against a court and an opposition. Anchored to real institutions with a never-fabricate rule.
- **Worldbuilding & games** — found a fictional nation, run it turn by turn, fire events and crises, and watch indicators and public trust move.
- **Civics & thinking** — see how an idea becomes law, and why a good idea still dies in committee.

## The roster (24 agents)

**Control plane** — `the-charter` (constitution & regime), `the-registrar` (the National Ledger: treasury, trust, indicators, timeline), `the-tribunal` (integrity + safety + evenhandedness gate).

**Heads** — `the-sovereign` (head of state), `the-premier` (head of government).

**Cabinet** — `the-treasury` (finance), `the-mint` (independent central bank), `the-economy` (industry/trade/labor), `the-interior` (home affairs, police, elections, borders), `the-envoy` (foreign affairs), `the-marshal` (defense), `the-cipher` (intelligence), `the-commons` (health/welfare/education/culture), `the-works` (infrastructure/energy/environment/digital), `the-chancery-of-justice` (justice ministry).

**Legislative** — `the-assembly` (parliament), `the-opposition` (loyal opposition).

**Judiciary** — `the-bench` (constitutional/supreme court, judicial review).

**Oversight & society** — `the-auditor` (audit, anti-corruption, ombudsman), `the-press` (free press), `the-citizenry` (public opinion, protest, turnout).

**Staff** — `the-mandarin` (civil-service policy analyst), `the-oracle` (foresight & strategy), `the-warden-of-crises` (emergency war-room).

## Modes

| Mode | What it does |
|---|---|
| **FOUND** | Constitute a state — pick the regime, write the charter, seed the nation. Run first. |
| **GOVERN** | Cabinet session — a problem in, a decided policy out, checked by court, opposition, press and people. |
| **LEGISLATE** | Bill → law — draft, cost, debate, amend, vote, assent, judicial review, enact. |
| **CRISIS** | War-room — a shock hits; rapid cross-ministry response under pressure. |
| **TICK** | Advance a turn — events fire, indicators update, decisions surface. The sandbox loop. |
| **AUDIT** | State of the nation — health, integrity, and trajectory. |
| **BRIEF** | Ask one minister — a quick single-seat consult. |

## Gates

- **Charter gate** (human) — confirm the constitution and regime before governing.
- **Enactment gate** (human) — approve any decision or law before it's written into the National Ledger.
- **Review gate** (hard, `the-tribunal`) — never-fabricate on real claims, evenhandedness present, simulation-boundary honored, coherence with the charter. Can BLOCK.

## Spine (non-negotiable)

- **Simulation boundary.** Statecraft governs a simulated society. When anchored to a real country, claims about real institutions must be non-fabricated (verified / assumed / simulated are labeled). It will not produce operational playbooks to repress, surveil, disenfranchise, or run disinformation against real people — authoritarian dynamics are analyzed as trade-offs in the sim, never as a real how-to.
- **Never fabricate.** Real statistics, laws, and figures are sourced or labeled; invented facts are labeled as simulation canon.
- **Evenhandedness.** Ideologies are modeled fairly; the checks (opposition, press, citizenry, court, auditor) are always voiced — even under non-democratic configs.

## Install

Accept the `.plugin` file when it appears in chat. Then start with, e.g.:

- `"Statecraft: found a mid-size EU-style parliamentary republic"` → FOUND
- `"govern: inflation is at 9% and the street is angry — what do we do?"` → GOVERN
- `"legislate a 4-day work week"` → LEGISLATE
- `"crisis: a neighboring state just cut our gas"` → CRISIS
- `"advance one turn"` → TICK

Bilingual: write in Romanian, get Romanian; write in English, get English. Internal artifacts are English.
