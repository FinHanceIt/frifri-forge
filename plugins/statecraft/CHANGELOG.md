# Changelog

## 1.0.0 — 2026-07-08
- Initial forge. Statecraft: a virtual state — 1 director skill (`statecraft`) + 24 specialist agents.
- Full separation of powers: heads of state & government, a 10-seat cabinet, an independent central bank, parliament + loyal opposition, a constitutional court.
- Oversight & society estates: independent audit/anti-corruption, a free press, and the citizenry.
- Staff brain: a neutral civil-service policy analyst, a foresight/strategy seat, and a crisis war-room.
- Seven modes: FOUND, GOVERN, LEGISLATE, CRISIS, TICK, AUDIT, BRIEF.
- Configurable regime library (constitutional democracy default) with a checks-and-balances matrix.
- Three gates: charter (human), enactment (human), and a hard integrity/safety review gate (the-tribunal).
- Spine: simulation-boundary, never-fabricate, evenhandedness. Bilingual RO/EN.
