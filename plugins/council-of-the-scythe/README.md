# Council of the Scythe

Portfolio governance — the anti-forge. Every other team starts things; this one forces
verdicts: **SHIP / KILL / FREEZE / IGNORE** on every item in the fleet, allocates a
100-point attention budget, and keeps score of Fri's predictions vs reality.

## Composition (1 skill + 3 agents)

| Unit | Seat | Owns |
|---|---|---|
| `scythe` (skill) | Convener | modes, routing, human gate, Council Record |
| `the-reaper` | Cold strategist | docket, rubric scores, verdicts, attention 100, anti-start GATE |
| `the-devils-advocate` | Red team | strongest attack on every keep AND every kill (steelman both ways) |
| `the-reckoner` | Ledger & calibration | Scythe-Ledger.md, falsifiable predictions, Brier scores Fri vs council, override audit |

## Modes

- **COUNCIL** — quarterly / on-demand full-fleet review → verdict on everything + attention allocation
- **JUDGMENT** — one item, fast: keep or kill
- **GATE** — anti-start check: a new idea enters only by naming what dies to fund it (default NO)
- **RECKONING** — resolve due predictions, calibration report, who was right

## Invocation

"Convoacă consiliul" · "portfolio review" · "ce omor și ce țin" · "merită să încep X?" ·
"cine a avut dreptat pe ultimele 90 de zile?"

## Spine

Never-fabricate (evidence or `UNKNOWN`, unknown reads as decay) · every verdict is a
falsifiable bet with a deadline · overrides logged, not argued · kills are wins ·
artifacts EN, interaction RO/EN.

## Files

- `Scythe-Ledger.md` (working folder) — roster, predictions, overrides, calibration, graveyard
- `council-{date}.md` — one record per session
