# Reference — The Physics of Life (the biophysics spine)

AevumForge's physics-of-life cluster reads aging and regeneration through **forces, fields,
energy, and information** — the layers above and beside molecular chemistry. Like the Hallmarks
map, this is a *framework and a source list*, not a source of live numbers: every specific value,
rate, or result must still come through `evidence-librarian`.

The agents on this spine: `structural-biophysicist` (molecule scale),
`biophysics-of-aging-specialist` (tissue/system scale + thermodynamics/information),
`bioelectricity-morphogenesis-specialist` (bioelectric pattern & regeneration),
`quantum-biophysicist` (the speculative quantum edge).

## Bioelectricity & morphogenesis (real, citable)
- **Vmem as a control variable** — resting membrane potential differs by cell state; ion channels,
  pumps, and gap junctions set and share it. Real, textbook electrophysiology.
- **Bioelectric pattern control** — a body of peer-reviewed work (e.g. Michael Levin and others)
  shows bioelectric gradients help specify anatomical pattern in models: two-headed planaria from
  gap-junction perturbation, ectopic eye induction in *Xenopus*, bioelectric roles in regeneration.
- **Honest limit:** the striking results are largely in high-regeneration model organisms
  (planaria, amphibians). Mammalian and human translation is early — always label the gap.

## Mechanobiology & the physics of tissues (real, citable)
- **Matrix mechanics** — extracellular-matrix stiffening and cross-linking with age; stiffness as
  a signal, not just scaffolding.
- **Mechanotransduction** — YAP/TAZ, integrins, and the cytoskeleton convert force into cell fate;
  implicated in senescence, stem-cell decline, and fibrosis.
- **Membrane biophysics** — lipid order, permeability, and the energetic cost of ion gradients.

## Thermodynamics & information (rigorous where labeled)
- **Open dissipative systems** — living bodies maintain order by exporting entropy (Schrödinger's
  *What is Life?* framing; non-equilibrium thermodynamics). Useful and real *as framing*.
- **Aging as information loss** — the idea that aging is progressive loss/corruption of biological
  information (notably epigenetic). A serious hypothesis; state what would make it *measurable*
  rather than treating the slogan as proof.

## Quantum biology (young field — tier everything)
- **Established:** proton/electron tunneling in enzyme catalysis and the electron-transport chain.
- **Active research:** the radical-pair mechanism (magnetoreception); possible spin effects on ROS.
- **Contested / speculative:** long-lived quantum coherence in warm biology; any direct tie to
  aging. Label `SPECULATIVE`. "Quantum healing / energy" longevity marketing is pseudoscience.

## Standing rules for this spine
1. **Framework, not statistics.** Every specific number or result comes through `evidence-librarian`.
2. **Tier honestly.** Model-organism ≠ mammal ≠ human; established ≠ active-research ≠ speculative.
   Name the tier on every claim.
3. **Physics informs, evidence decides.** A physical rationale is a `HYPOTHESIS` until measured.
4. **No pseudoscience laundering.** "Frequency," "vibration," and "energy" cures are symbol only
   unless real evidence exists; route health claims to `bioethics-safety-warden`.
