# Reference — The 12 Hallmarks of Aging (the Scientia spine)

The institute organizes aging biology around the **Hallmarks of Aging** framework. It is a
*descriptive framework*, not a proven causal law — treat it as the shared map, not as dogma.

**Sources (real, citable):**
- López-Otín C, Blasco MA, Partridge L, Serrano M, Kroemer G. *The Hallmarks of Aging.* **Cell**,
  2013 — the original nine.
- López-Otín C, Blasco MA, Partridge L, Serrano M, Kroemer G. *Hallmarks of Aging: An Expanding
  Universe.* **Cell**, 2023 — expanded to twelve.

A trait qualifies as a hallmark (per the authors) when it: (1) manifests with age, (2)
accelerates aging when experimentally worsened, and (3) can decelerate/stop/reverse aging when
therapeutically targeted.

## The twelve (2023)

**Primary hallmarks — the damage:**
1. **Genomic instability** — accumulating DNA damage & mutations. → `telomere-genome-guardian`
2. **Telomere attrition** — erosion of chromosome ends, replicative limits. → `telomere-genome-guardian`
3. **Epigenetic alterations** — drift in methylation/chromatin, loss of epigenetic information.
   → `epigenetics-reprogramming-specialist`
4. **Loss of proteostasis** — failing protein quality control, aggregation. → `proteostasis-autophagy-specialist`
5. **Disabled macroautophagy** *(new in 2023)* — impaired self-clearance of damaged components.
   → `proteostasis-autophagy-specialist`

**Antagonistic hallmarks — the responses that turn harmful:**
6. **Deregulated nutrient-sensing** — mTOR/AMPK/IGF-1/sirtuin imbalance. → `mitochondria-metabolism-specialist`
7. **Mitochondrial dysfunction** — bioenergetic decline, ROS, mtDNA damage. → `mitochondria-metabolism-specialist`
8. **Cellular senescence** — accumulation of arrested, SASP-secreting cells. → `senescence-senolytics-specialist`

**Integrative hallmarks — the systemic phenotype:**
9. **Stem-cell exhaustion** — declining regenerative reserve. → `stem-cell-regeneration-specialist`
10. **Altered intercellular communication** — disrupted signaling between cells/tissues.
    → `immuno-inflammaging-specialist`
11. **Chronic inflammation** *(new in 2023)* — "inflammaging." → `immuno-inflammaging-specialist`
12. **Dysbiosis** *(new in 2023)* — microbiome imbalance affecting host aging.
    → `immuno-inflammaging-specialist` (with `evidence-librarian` for microbiome claims)

## How to use this map
- The `gerontologist` sets `HALLMARK FOCUS` at the start of any aging inquiry and routes.
- The hallmarks are **entangled**, not independent dials — an intervention on one ripples to
  others. State those couplings explicitly.
- **Lifespan ≠ healthspan.** Most robust interventions (mTOR inhibition, caloric restriction)
  are strongest in short-lived models; the human evidence is thinner. Always name the tier.
- Every specific number, effect size, or trial result cited against a hallmark must come through
  the `evidence-librarian`. This document is the *framework*, not a source of live statistics.
