# AevumForge

*aevum* (Latin): lifespan, an age, duration.

A virtual **longevity & cellular-regeneration research institute** for Claude (Cowork / Claude
Code): **1 director skill + 31 specialist agents** organized into two co-equal hemispheres and
two gates, pointed at one Great Work — *understanding the molecular biology of aging,
regeneration, and rejuvenation well enough to reason about real interventions.*

It is a tool for **understanding and reasoning** — mechanisms, literature, hypotheses, study
design, and the honest appraisal of longevity claims. It is **not** a wet-lab protocol service.

## The design in one idea

Two traditions of inquiry, co-equal in *asking*, unequal in *claiming*:

- **SCIENTIA** — empirical science (molecular biology, genetics, gene-editing, biophysics, chemistry, the 12 Hallmarks of Aging).
- **HERMETICA** — the alchemical/hermetic tradition, taken seriously as a source of **symbol,
  analogy, and directed imagination**.

One law binds both — **the Law of the Two Books:** the Book of Nature (evidence) alone decides
what is *true about the body*; the Book of Art (the hermetic corpus) can say *where to look*,
never *what is there*. A hermetic idea enters the record only as a **tagged hypothesis** and
earns "finding" status only through evidence. That is how the esoteric layer carries real,
heavy weight **and** nothing is ever fabricated.

## Use it

Say things like:

- "Help me understand how partial reprogramming could reverse cell age" → **INQUIRE**: the
  right specialists explain, sourced, with honest evidence tiers.
- "Find me a fresh angle on clearing senescent cells" / "dă-mi un unghi nou pe senescență" →
  **GREAT WORK**: both hemispheres generate, the weaver bridges symbol→hypothesis, evidence +
  skeptic + both gates, then ranked hypotheses.
- "Is NMN actually worth it?" / "chiar merită NMN?" → **APPRAISE**: evidence + specialist +
  skeptic + warden → honest verdict.
- "How would you test hypothesis H?" → **DESIGN**: a conceptual study outline (not a protocol).

Interaction is bilingual (RO/EN); all internal artifacts are English.

## House rules baked in

- **Never fabricate — either Book.** No invented studies, numbers, or citations; no invented
  hermetic quotes. Unknown is stated. Every empirical claim carries an evidence status.
- **The firewall.** Symbols cross to science only as tagged, falsifiable hypotheses, decided by
  evidence — never asserted as fact.
- **Two gates.** Nothing labeled a finding or plan ships without the integrity PASS, and the
  safety PASS whenever content touches application.
- **The boundary.** Understanding, hypotheses, and study design — not wet-lab protocols, not
  dual-use uplift, not individualized medical advice, not literal toxic elixirs.

## Not medical advice

AevumForge explains and reasons about science. It does not diagnose, prescribe, or advise any
individual on their health or longevity. For personal decisions, consult qualified professionals.
