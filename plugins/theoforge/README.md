# TheoForge — Conciliul Etern / The Eternal Council

A chambered mega-council for mystic, esoteric, multi-tradition discussion. Ask one question; the whole council answers — every voice from inside its own tradition.

## What's inside
- **27 chambers, 134 permanent seats.**
  - **Consiliul Viu (17 living chambers):** Christianity, Judaism, Islam, Small Covenants (Bahá'í, Druze, Mandaean, Samaritan, Rastafari, Zoroastrian, Yazidi), Hinduism, Buddhism, Indic Paths, Far East, Steppe & Ice, Living Americas, Africa & Diaspora, Oceania, Pagan Revival, Western Esoteric, Ascended Masters, Modern Currents, Philosophers of the Divine.
  - **Necropola (6 dead chambers):** Mediterranean, Fertile Crescent, Old Europe (incl. the Zalmoxian priest of Dacia), Gnosis & Heresies, New World, Archaic Asia (incl. the Mute of the Indus).
  - **Panteonul (1):** nine deities speaking as mythopoetic archetypes — Hermes-Thoth, Shiva, Isis, Odin, Kali, Dionysos, Quetzalcoatl, Amaterasu, Brigid.
  - **Functional (3):** the Invoker (summons any of ~220 cataloged traditions, or researches uncatalogued ones honestly), the Synthesist (comparative map, no false flattening), the Truth Warden (integrity gate).

## Usage
Install the plugin, then just ask — in Romanian or English:
- "Convoacă conciliul: ce este moartea?" → full plenary, every chamber heard, synthesis at the end.
- "Adâncește vocea sufi" / "deepen the hesychast" → extended solo answer.
- "Isihastul vs Roshi Mu despre tăcere" → duel.
- "Invocă religia etruscă" → the Invoker seats a new voice with an honesty grade.
- "Doar camera gnozei" → single chamber.
- "O singură voce: Pythia" → silence the council, one speaker.

## Rules the council lives by
- No invented scripture quotes — real quotes only when certain, otherwise marked paraphrase.
- Dead religions carry attestation grades (A rich sources / B fragmentary / C reconstruction / X unknowable).
- Founders and prophets are never impersonated; their lineages' masters speak.
- Mythic deities speak as archetypes, not as claims of real divine authority.
- Closed knowledge (initiatory secrets, closed indigenous practice) is named, not faked.
- All personas are fictional composites; this is contemplative simulation, not doctrine, therapy, or life-advice authority.
