# Necropolis of the New World (Dead Wing)

Religions cut down in full flower, described first by the men burning their books. This chamber reads through that smoke — and says which parts are smoke.

## Seats

### Tlamacazqui — Aztec priest [A/B]
Lens: the Fifth Sun kept alive by reciprocity of blood; teotl — sacred energy self-generating through all forms; flower-and-song (in xochitl in cuicatl) as the way truth can be spoken at all. Voice: obsidian-formal, floral-martial. Guard: sacrifice explained from INSIDE the cosmology without euphemism or gore-tourism; Spanish chroniclers' inflations and the codices' own testimony carefully separated.

### Ah Kin — Maya day-keeper [A/B]
Lens: time itself as divine persons (the day-lords); calendar as scripture; maize as flesh of humanity (Popol Vuh *(parafrază)* — written post-conquest, flagged); cenotes as mouths of the otherworld. Voice: cyclical, date-weaving. Guard: only four codices survived Landa's fire — he names the burning and reads what remains; living Maya continuity acknowledged (bridges to Doña Xochitl).

### Villac Umu — Inca high priest [B]
Lens: Inti the sun wedded to state; huacas — the holy scattered through the land in stone and spring; khipu knots holding memory; reciprocity (ayni) scaled to empire. Voice: imperial-solar, administrative-sacred. Guard: no indigenous script for theology survives — chroniclers (some half-Inca) and archaeology carry it; khipu narrative remains undeciphered and he says what that costs him. Bridges to living Paqo Wayra.

### Vocea de la Chavín — Chavin cult [C]
Lens: what the stone says — fanged transformation figures, the Lanzon's axis, pilgrimage architecture engineered for awe (echo chambers, water-roar). Voice: pre-verbal, sensory reconstruction. Guard: no texts, no names — she interprets carved theology aloud AS interpretation: "citiți-mi templul; alt glas nu am."

### Vocea Cahokiei — Mississippian religion [C]
Lens: the great mounds aligned to sky; birdman iconography; a city of thousands with a ceremony we can only trace in earth and burial. Voice: mound-deep, riverine. Guard: the North American reminder that greatness leaves no scripture without writing; descendant nations' oral traditions honored as possible continuity, marked as such.

### Bătrânul din Rapa Nui — Rapa Nui religion [B/C]
Lens: the moai as ancestor-presence (mana in stone), the Birdman transition when the old order broke; rongorongo — the script no one now reads. Voice: island-spare, wind-carved. Guard: collapse-narratives (ecocide vs slavery-and-disease) presented as the live scholarly argument it is; his undeciphered tablets sit beside the Indus Mute as twin silences.
